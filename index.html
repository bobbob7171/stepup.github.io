Welcome — this is a guided prototype

StepUp Campus turns trips you're already making around campus into short active routes. Here's how to explore it:

1
Walk the journey using the numbered steps on the left — or just tap through the screens yourself.
2
Everything is live: flip the Step-free toggle, pick different routes, and watch your points update for real.
3
Open "See the full flow" any time for the big-picture map of how the whole app works.
